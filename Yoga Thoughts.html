Monologue: Yoga Thoughts

Nora in a virtual yoga class. She is doing it together with a friend via Facetime. Yoga is on the TV. Friend is directly at the camera.

Posted on August 24, 2021

NORA

This is gonna be great. I already feel better sitting here in this position. Don’t you? (friend doesn’t answer, she’s focused on the yoga class) Oh right. Sorry, sorry.  (The class begins. Stretch up, roll down, down-dog, cobra.) This is really great for my inner growth, like I can feel my back stretching and my ego losing his power. Yeah, he’s a guy. I don’t know why (she laughs, no response, she keeps going. Flow of sun salutations warm up). You know they say that a tree keeps growing as long as it is living. I would like to be like that, you know? Grow as long as I am alive? I don’t know… Ouch. My thong is just… damn, it wants to be more than friends if you know what I mean… (No response. She laughs to relieve the silence). I want to be multidimensional. Wear lots of hats? Like when people are trying to define me I trick them and boom! Who even am I? And they have to figure it out all over again (balancing star pose, her entire body shakes) It’s a way to keep people interested, you know? I even… (pose is hard) I even get bored of myself every once in a while and then I get a haircut or wear a death metal t-shirt and people get so confused. It’s my favorite part, their faces! (looks at the camera) Wait you froze! Kim are you there? I can’t see if you left or froze? Should I wait for you or…? Oh there you are, ha! Nevermind. Wow, you’re good at this. Can you hear me? Oh… you can… cool, cool. Damn, my timelapse got cut off! Let me just take a selfie then. (She takes a selfie and types) Hashtag yogathoughts. Great… well… I think I need a break. I’m gonna go make a grilled cheese! You want any? Haha! Just kidding! Alright.. See you around.   (Turns camera off)
